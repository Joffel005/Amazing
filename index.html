Benefits of Drinking Coffee (No Sugars and Creamers):

More Energy

Caffeine in coffee blocks adenosine receptors in the brain, increasing energy levels and productivity.

Weight Management

Coffee may aid in weight management by increasing physical activity levels and altering the body's fat storage, leading to decreased body fat in both men and women.

Increase Peformance

Caffeine in coffee may improve physical performance by increasing muscle contraction force and endurance during exercise, as well as reducing perceived exertion.







Excelsa

Excelsa coffee beans boast a balanced flavor profile with a medium body also offering a smooth and satisfying cup with hints of fruit and nuts.

Liberica

Liberica coffee beans are known for their unique flavor profile, featuring notes of fruit, florals, and spices, with medium body and its sweetness.

Arabica

Arabica coffee beans offer complex flavor profile, notes of fruit, chocolate, wine, and a bright acidity that adds depth and character to every cup.

Robusta

Robusta coffee beans provide a rich and full-bodied experience, delivering a bold and intense flavor profile with hints of chocolate and a slight bitterness.
